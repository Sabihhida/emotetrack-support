Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

EmoteTrack is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your data is collected, used, and protected when you use the EmoteTrack application.

1. Information We Collect

EmoteTrack stores only the mood entries, notes, and analytics data you create within the app. No personal identity information is required or collected.

2. How Your Data Is Used

Your mood logs and analytics are used solely for displaying insights within the app. We do not share, sell, or use your information for advertising.

3. Data Storage

All data is stored securely on your device or your iCloud account if enabled. You have full control over your data.

4. Third‑Party Services

EmoteTrack does not integrate any third‑party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

5. Your Rights

You can delete your mood data anytime directly from the app. Uninstalling the app also removes all stored data from your device.
